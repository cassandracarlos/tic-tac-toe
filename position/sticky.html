Chapter 1: Verse 1:0 — Fundament

This chapter introduces the setting, a gas giant called Fundament, with a vast ocean containing many continents. The proto-Hive species live on these continents, and their society is feudal and divided. The main characters, Aurash, Sathona, and Xi Ro, are the daughters of the Osmium King. The Osmium King, obsessed with the disaster that a looming "God-Wave" might bring, is overthrown and killed by the Helium Drinkers. The sisters are forced to flee.

Chapter 2: Verse 1:1 — Predators

Aurash, Sathona, and Xi Ro make a pact to avenge their father's death and reclaim their kingdom. They sail away on their ship, the Needle. On their journey, they encounter various predators of the deep ocean, reflecting the hostile environment of Fundament. Their resolve to grow stronger and gain power hardens as they face these challenges.

Chapter 3: Verse 1:2 — The Syzygy

The sisters learn of the Syzygy, a catastrophic event where the moons of Fundament align, causing a massive tidal wave that could destroy all life on the planet. They realize they need to seek out greater power to survive. Sathona is guided by the whispers of her dead father's familiar, urging her to dive deeper into the secrets of Fundament.

Chapter 4: Verse 1:3 — The Oath

The sisters discover an ancient ship, the Needle, which can take them to the center of Fundament. There, they hope to find the secrets of the fabled Worms, powerful beings that can grant them the strength they seek. They swear an oath to each other to delve into the unknown and gain the power to reshape their destiny.

Chapter 5: Verse 1:4 — Syzygy

As the Syzygy approaches, the sisters venture into the depths of Fundament. They encounter the Leviathan, a creature that warns them against seeking the Worms' power, claiming it will bring ruin. However, Sathona's familiar urges them to proceed, promising unimaginable power. The sisters decide to ignore the Leviathan's warnings and continue their journey into the darkness.

Chapter 6: Verse 1:5 — Needle and Worm

The sisters find the Worm Gods, who promise them power in exchange for servitude. They accept the deal, transforming into powerful beings: Aurash becomes Auryx, Sathona becomes Savathûn, and Xi Ro becomes Xivu Arath. They gain new abilities and a new purpose: to conquer and destroy to feed their Worms.

Chapter 7: Verse 1:6 — The Dive

Auryx and his sisters lead their people in a campaign of destruction, driven by their Worms' hunger. The more they destroy, the stronger they become. They begin to understand the true nature of the power they have been granted and the cost of their new existence.

Chapter 8: Verse 1:7 — Leviathan Rises

The Leviathan, who warned the sisters against the Worms, rises to confront them again. Despite its power, the Leviathan cannot stop them. The sisters, now fully embracing their Hive nature, defeat it and continue their conquest.

Chapter 9: Verse 1:8 — King of Shapes

Auryx learns the Sword Logic, a philosophy that might makes right and that only through destruction can one prove their worth. He uses this philosophy to challenge and overthrow the reigning powers of Fundament, establishing himself as the King of the Hive.

Chapter 10: Verse 1:9 — Crusaders

Auryx, Savathûn, and Xivu Arath lead the Hive on a crusade across the stars, spreading destruction wherever they go. They encounter various civilizations and species, all of which fall before their might. Their power grows as they continue to feed their Worms with the destruction they wreak.

Chapter 11: Verse 2:0 — Immortals

The Hive solidify their power, becoming feared across the cosmos. They achieve near-immortality through their pact with the Worms, continually growing in strength and influence. This chapter explores their ongoing conquest and the expansion of their dark empire.

Chapter 12: Verse 2:1 — Conquerors

Auryx, now known as Oryx, the Taken King, further refines the Sword Logic and uses it to guide his conquests. He begins to seek out other powerful beings to either destroy or subjugate. His sisters, Savathûn and Xivu Arath, also grow in power and influence.

Chapter 13: Verse 2:2 — The High War

Oryx and his siblings engage in a high war, testing their strength against each other and other powerful entities in the universe. This chapter highlights their relentless pursuit of power and the destruction they leave in their wake.

Chapter 14: Verse 2:3 — Tribute

The Hive develop a system of tribute, where lesser Hive must offer power and essence to their superiors. This system ensures a constant flow of strength to the leaders of the Hive, particularly Oryx, Savathûn, and Xivu Arath.

Chapter 15: Verse 2:4 — The Partition of Death

Oryx discovers the Deep, a source of dark power that further enhances his abilities. He learns to take control of other beings, bending them to his will and increasing his influence. This chapter delves into Oryx's growing mastery over death and the forces of the Deep.
